Get to Know Me

What’s your professional experience?

(I am a UI Designer with a background in Mechanical Engineering.) As a Mechanical Engineer, I loved finding and developing solutions to problems. While engineering has some aspects of design, I wanted to expand past the technical aspects and focus on how my designs impact others. After much research, UI Design appealed to me through its user-centered approach to solving problems. I then decided to pursue this career path. I received a UI Design certificate through CareerFoundry after months of training. Through the training process, I became proficient in design thinking, user research, and user testing.

What’s your proudest accomplishment?

Completing the UI Design Certification program, of course!

How do you spend your free time?

I love listening to NPR podcasts. Some of my favorites include How I Built This, Invisibilia, and Throughline. I also try to read mystery books whenever I can. As a creative at heart, I enjoy painting, drawing, and creating digital art. I even started an Instagram to showcase some of my latest pieces. Check it out here if you’d like!

What’s a fun fact about you?

I have two patents with my name on them! In my previous role as a Patent Examiner, I reviewed dozens of patent applications. Two of them met all of the qualifications to be published as official patents.
